Senior Project based on Unity

This gameâ€™s genres are serious and simulation game. It contains several modes that are single, multiplayer and AR examination mods.

Story is based on the character who tries to clean hidden research center. The character has to kill all enemies in the facility.

In the single mode, there are two types of levels. The first one is battle missions second one is acrobatic missions. In battle missions, players can control aircraft, destroy targets, evacuate people and complete missions. Also, NPCs are added to battle missions, and these are capable of patrol, tracking and shooting. In acrobatic missions, they should control aircraft over the city and reach target checkpoints.

In the multiplayer mode, players fight with other players and try to gain more points by shooting them. In this mode, players can only use F16 for now.

In the AR examination mode, players can place aircraft in any environment and place them in their daily life to examine them in a more detailed way. In this mode, players can gain lots of information about the aircraft and their parts with the help of hotspots and information panels.
